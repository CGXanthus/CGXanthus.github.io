About Our Team - Richard

Hi, I’m Richard. I’m 24 years old, born and raised in Chicago. Through my time at Year Up Chicago, I have learned many technical skills that I hope are displayed on this site. I have developed a desire also for project management and have a professional understanding of several Microsoft Office applications. For example, I have learned how to use Outlook, Excel, Word, and MS Project. I really took to Excel more than any other application and have decided to display my knowledge through the site. I thankfully also improved on my public speaking abilities. I use to get nervous in front of a crowd but thanks to the practice and lessons given in my Business Communication class, that is no longer the case. I now look forward to speaking to an audience and being affective at it. These skills are some that I’m proud to have because I know I will need them to accomplish my future goals and dreams.
