Introduction

Business Communications and Tech skills are important because, they both play a part in creating not only a professional career but also a successful career. Business communications equips you with the proper tools on how to communicate in corporate America. Some of the skills you learn are: How to send professional emails, giving an effective elevator pitch, and creating professional resumes and cover letters. All of these skills are important because, they have a huge impact on not only your position within a company but also whether a company will hire you or not. Tech is an extremely important skill because, a major part of working in a business environment is communicating via email. In situations where your computer won’t power on or, won’t connect to the internet you need the correct type of skills to solve the problem quickly. The educational methods that we will be using are useful/helpful because, they will not only give off the visual aspect of learning the skills. They will also have a step by step breakdown so that the viewers can easily follow along with us.
